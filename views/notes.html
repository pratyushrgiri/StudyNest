Momentum & Impulse

Edited 2 hours ago

## Definitions

Momentum is the product of an object's mass and velocity. Impulse is the change in momentum produced by a force over time. 

p = m · v
J = Δp = F · Δt

### Key points

  • Momentum is a vector — direction matters.
  • In a closed system, total momentum is conserved.
  • Impulse-momentum theorem links force, time, and Δp.

  3. Data table
